          About Up Helly Aa Shetland's Fire Festival Season

          Shetland's Up Helly Aa festivals

          Between January and March every year, communities across Shetland come together for a series of spectacular and unique fiery celebrations.

          Shetland's Up Helly Aa festivals are partly a celebration of Shetland Viking past, but also a triumphant demonstration of islanders' skill, spirit and sense of community. They are true community endeavours, bringing together people from all generations and backgrounds, from across the islands, to plan and celebrate.
